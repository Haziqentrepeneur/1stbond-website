The Problem

Millions Meet Strangers
With No Real Accountability.

Marketplaces, apps, and platforms bring people together — but leave the trust entirely up to you. That gap is where things go wrong.

Fake identities and impersonation go unchecked on most platforms
Payment disputes with no third-party accountability or protection
No system that connects identity, communication, and meetup safety in one place
Unsafe meetup conditions with no real-time monitoring or emergency support
No proof or evidence when something goes wrong after the meetup
Last-minute no-shows with zero consequences or recourse
The Fix?
A single platform that ties together identity, communication, location, payments, and emergency safety — built from the ground up for real-world interactions. That's 1STBOND.
How It Works

A Complete Trust Journey —
Every Step of the Way.

1STBOND structures safety before, during, and after every meetup — so you never have to figure it out on your own.

1
Account & Identity
Create Your Account & Verify Your Identity

Join 1STBOND and complete mandatory ID verification through Stripe Identity. This isn't optional — it's the foundation. Every user on the platform has confirmed they are a real, accountable person before they can interact with anyone else.

Stripe Identity Mandatory Verification No Fake Accounts
2
Connect
Message Directly on Platform

Find who you need and communicate within the app. Discuss the item, service, or meetup details without ever leaving 1STBOND. Keeping the conversation on-platform means everything stays connected to verified identities.

On-Platform Messaging Tied to Verified Identity
3
Pre-Meet
Face Scan Before Every Meetup

Before an in-person meetup begins, both parties complete a live face scan. This confirms the person showing up is the same verified identity attached to the account — not just a one-time badge, but active confirmation at the moment it matters most.

Live Face Scan Real-Time Confirmation Anti-Impersonation
4
Premium Meetup
Activate Live Safety Monitoring

When a Premium Meetup is active, the app uses both parties' locations to intelligently guide the meetup from start to finish. It recommends when you should head out based on distance and travel time, and automatically checks you in as "On the Way" once your GPS confirms you're moving toward the meetup spot. When you arrive, the other person is notified automatically — no manual check-in needed. The meetup closes on its own once the platform detects both parties have gone their separate ways.

Live Monitoring Auto ETA Tracking One Premium Unlocks Both
5
Emergency
Panic Button — Live Location & Audio for Your Emergency Contact

If something goes wrong, Premium users can hit the panic button. Instantly, their emergency contact receives their live location and can tune into a live audio stream from the scene — giving someone who cares about you real-time awareness when it matters most. This isn't a symbolic button. It's a real safety line.

Panic Button Live Location Share Live Audio Stream Premium Feature
6
Transact
Secure Payment Within the Platform

Pay and get paid through 1STBOND's protected payment layer. Payments are tied to verified identities and the meetup record — reducing disputes, increasing accountability, and giving both parties peace of mind around the transaction.

Secure Payments Tied to Identity Dispute Protection

Security Architecture

Engineered for Privacy.
Built for Trust.

Every layer of 1STBOND is designed around one principle: trust isn't an extra feature — it's infrastructure.

Stripe Identity Verification

Every user is verified through Stripe's trusted identity infrastructure — not a checkbox, but a real confirmation that you are who you say you are.

Secure Payment Layer

Payments happen inside a protected environment tied to verified identities. No more off-platform Venmo links or cash-only arrangements with no accountability.

Privacy-First Panic System

Emergency alerts share your live location with your designated contact — never the other party's identity. Your emergency information is yours alone.

Zero Data Selling Policy

Location data, identity data, and interaction records are never sold or monetized. Your information is used to protect you — nothing else.

Mandatory Meetup Protection

Safety monitoring doesn't require you to remember to turn it on. It activates automatically during the meetup window and ends when both parties leave the zone.

Panic Button — Live Location & Audio

Premium users can trigger the panic button at any moment. Their emergency contact immediately receives their live location and can tune into a live audio stream from the scene — real-time awareness, not just a notification.
